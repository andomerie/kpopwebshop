About Us

  1. Home/
  2. About Us

We are two best friends, with the vision that music creates meaning and brings people together. We want to offer a cozy and comfy Kpop business in a space of excitement and therefore opened The Kpop during the winter of 2023.

We wanted a Kpop store that would shine with joy. That's why we made our webshop like we did - We dream of opening a space later on where you can have a chat with other fans, meet new fans and find your next inspo.

We are grateful to be able to open up The Kpop. And we are excited to present you all a webshop that has been set up with simplicity and aesthetics so you can find your favorite albums in an easy and pleasing way.

Our goal is to continue our visual music universe together with you with events and good vibes.

We look forward to every day where we can meet you for a chat about what's trending within Kpop, about personal bias, advice for gifts and much more. We hope you feel home here and want to stay with us, and come along for the ride.

Best wishes, Rebekka and Lise-Lotte

Rebekka

Rebekka is one of the founders of The Kpop and ran the store in the evenings after work. She's been listening to Kpop since 2015 and has built the store alongside getting more knowledge about Kpop. Her favorite artists are GOT7, Stray Kids and TXT.

Lise-Lotte

Lise-Lotte is one of the founders of The Kpop and ran the store in the evenings after School. She's been listening to Kpop since 2010 and has built the store alongside getting more knowledge about newer Kpop. Her favorite artists are Seventeen, Itzy and Mamamoo.
